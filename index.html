OUR PROJECT

At GO//SOLAR, we take pride in offering a diverse range of high-quality solar inverter products that empower you to harness the full potential of solar energy.

Grid-Tied Solar Inverters

Our grid-tied solar inverters are the perfect solution for homeowners and businesses looking to maximize their energy savings. These inverters seamlessly integrate with your existing power grid, allowing you to efficiently use solar energy and even sell excess power back to the grid.

Off-Grid Solar Inverters

For those seeking energy independence or living in remote areas without a reliable power grid, our off-grid solar inverters provide a dependable solution. These inverters store excess solar energy in batteries for use during cloudy days or at night, ensuring a continuous power supply.

Hybrid Solar Inverters

Our hybrid solar inverters combine the benefits of both grid-tied and off-grid inverters. These versatile systems offer the flexibility to switch between grid-connected and off-grid modes, allowing you to make the most of your solar power while maintaining a backup power source.
